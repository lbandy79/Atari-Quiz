In the 1980's, kids had to use their imaginations when playing Atari. Pong was two blocks with another bouncing in-between. What was that suppose to be? A tennis match? A weird game of catch? A metaphor of American politics?
Luckily there was box art to help players know what all those pixels represented. But even the box art could be deceiving.
Can you guess the game from its cover? You'll get a point for everyone one you get right! See if you can get a Hi-Score! .
